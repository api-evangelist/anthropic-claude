Anthropic launched Claude Tag, a Slack integration that lets teams tag @Claude to delegate tasks while it works asynchronously, accessing selected channels, tools, and codebases while remembering relevant context. Anthropic notes that 65 percent of its product team's code is created by its internal version of Claude Tag across engineering, support, and data analysis workflows.
